Research Experience
======
* COAST Guidewire Robot, Georgia Institute of Technology, Aug 2024 – Present
  * Developed a novel robotic guidewire system for minimally invasive cardiovascular interventions.
  * Implemented control algorithms for precise navigation and manipulation inside blood vessels.
  * Collaborated with interdisciplinary team to integrate sensors and actuators.

* Hydraulically Actuated Robot, Southern University of Science and Technology, Jan 2023 – Jun 2024
  * Designed and prototyped a hydraulically actuated soft robot for medical applications.
  * Conducted experiments to evaluate robot performance and safety.
  * Applied finite element analysis for structural optimization.

* Movement Analysis using IMU Sensors, Southern University of Science and Technology, Jun 2022 – Dec 2022
  * Collected and analyzed biomechanical data using wearable inertial measurement units.
  * Developed algorithms for real-time motion tracking and classification.
  * Validated system accuracy through clinical trials.

* Motion Capture for Rehabilitation, Southern University of Science and Technology, Sep 2021 – May 2022
  * Implemented motion capture techniques to assess patient rehabilitation progress.
  * Collaborated with clinicians to tailor rehabilitation protocols.
  * Presented findings at international conferences.

* Navigation System for Surgeries, Southern University of Science and Technology, Jun 2021 – Aug 2021
  * Assisted in the development of a navigation system for surgical procedures.
  * Integrated imaging data with robotic control interfaces.
  * Supported system testing and validation.

* Center of Mass Estimation, Southern University of Science and Technology, Sep 2020 – Dec 2020
  * Developed algorithms to estimate human center of mass using sensor data.
  * Applied methods to improve balance assessment in clinical settings.
